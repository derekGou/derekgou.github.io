Co-founder and Co-lead - SproutHacks

SproutHacks is a beginner-friendly, free-to-attend hackathon for high school students, held in-person in Waterloo. We provide youth of all backgrounds with an opportunity to build a project while learning from industry professionals and like-minded peers. Our novel hackathon structure cultivates an ideation-focused environment.

As the co-lead, Derek has raised thousands of dollars in funding for the event. He is responsible for finances, as well as managing emails, venue, sponsor packages, and project proposals, while helping out with web development, logistics, and graphic design.
